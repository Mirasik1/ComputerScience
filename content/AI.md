# Spheres of use
---
### **Indutsry**
---
- industrial multitasking robots
### **Education**
---
- AI can ensure that educational software is
personalized for students. This system emphasizes each
student’s needs, highlighting specific topics students are weak
in and repeating subjects they haven’t mastered.
AI can help teachers create different content types that make
teaching and learning more comfortable.
### **Medicine**
---
- AI can help to make better and faster diagnoses. Online virtual health assistants and chatbots to help patients.
### **Gaming**
---
- AI in gaming is about creating more responsive, adaptive & challenging games. Non-player characters’ behavior is determined by artificial intelligence algorithms and engines. The game AI can generate the game landscape or the game world as you go through the game world.
### **Society**
---
- Chatbots in banking. Voice assitstants. Image/Face Recognition.
